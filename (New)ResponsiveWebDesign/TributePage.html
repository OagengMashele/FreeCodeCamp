SATOSHI KON(今敏)

Basic Info about tribute

Satoshi Kon (今敏, Kon Satoshi, October 12, 1963 – August 24, 2010[3]) was a Japanese film director, animator, screenwriter and manga artist from Sapporo, Hokkaidō and a member of the Japanese Animation Creators Association (JAniCA).[4] He was a graduate of the Graphic Design department of the Musashino Art University. He is best known for his acclaimed anime films:Perfect Blue (1997),Millennium Actress (2001),Tokyo Godfathers (2003) and Paprika (2006).
